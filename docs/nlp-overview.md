# Neuro-Linguistic Programming — One-Page Field Map

*Scope: the Bandler/Grinder behavioral-change discipline (1970s–present), not computational NLP/LLMs. Practitioner orientation, 2003–2008 cohort.*

## Origins
Created early-to-mid 1970s by Richard Bandler and John Grinder (UC Santa Cruz) by modeling three therapists: Fritz Perls (Gestalt), Virginia Satir (family systems), Milton Erickson (hypnosis). Core premise: subjective experience has a structure of neurology, language, and learned patterns, and that structure can be modeled and changed. Foundational texts: *The Structure of Magic I/II*, *Patterns of the Hypnotic Techniques of Milton H. Erickson*, *Frogs into Princes*.

## Core toolkit (the part that carries through)
Rapport/matching, representational systems (VAKOG), submodalities, anchoring, the Meta Model (language precision), the Milton Model (artful vagueness), reframing, well-formed outcomes, presuppositions, perceptual positions, strategies (TOTE), parts work. These remain the durable, transferable skills; the testable claims around them did not survive scrutiny.

## Branches (post-1980 fragmentation, accelerated by the Bandler/Grinder trademark litigation)
- **Society of NLP (Bandler)** — Design Human Engineering (DHE), Neuro-Hypnotic Repatterning.
- **New Code NLP (Grinder & Judith DeLozier)** — fixes classic-code flaws: state-first work, multiple perceptual positions, unconscious-competence games.
- **NLPU / Robert Dilts** — Neurological (Logical) Levels, Sleight of Mouth, modeling methodology, strategy elicitation.
- **NLP Comprehensive (Steve & Connirae Andreas)** — Core Transformation, later the Wholeness Work.
- **Time Line Therapy (Tad James)**; **Neuro-Semantics / Meta-States (L. Michael Hall)**.
- **Mass-market spinoff** — Tony Robbins' Neuro-Associative Conditioning (NAC).

## Current trends (to 2026)
- **Brand decline, technique diffusion.** "NLP" as a standalone label carries a pseudoscience reputation (rep-systems and eye-accessing-cue claims were empirically falsified; no robust efficacy evidence). The *methods* migrated and survive unbranded.
- **Coaching is the main carrier.** ICF-style and executive coaching absorbed rapport, outcome framing, presuppositional questioning, and reframing as standard craft.
- **Clean Language & Symbolic Modeling** (David Grove; Tompkins & Lawley) — the most credible offshoot, rigorous metaphor work with low practitioner imposition.
- **Somatic/trauma turn.** Crude anchoring is being replaced by nervous-system literacy: Polyvagal theory, Somatic Experiencing, Brainspotting, EMDR adjacency.
- **Parts work mainstreamed.** IFS (Internal Family Systems) now occupies the territory NLP parts integration and Core Transformation pioneered, with broader clinical acceptance.
- **Empirical re-grounding.** Memory reconsolidation science (Coherence Therapy, Bruce Ecker) gives a mechanism for what submodality shifts and reimprinting did intuitively.

## New and adjacent practices integrating NLP
- **AI/digital coaching** — chatbots and apps using outcome framing, reframing, and Milton-pattern language; persuasion and UX/conversion design borrowing Meta/Milton structures.
- **Neuro-Semantics, Havening, PSYCH-K** — successor systems layering newer neuroscience framing onto NLP-style interventions.
- **Persuasion/influence** — overlap with Cialdini-style influence and modern sales methodology.
- **Naming collision** — the acronym now defaults to computational natural-language processing; expect to disambiguate constantly.

## Practitioner takeaway
Treat NLP as a **pattern library, not a theory**. The skills (rapport, language precision, reframing, outcome work, state management) transfer cleanly into coaching, hypnotherapy, and somatic practice. Drop the falsified mechanistic claims, pair the techniques with reconsolidation- and polyvagal-informed models, and rebrand under whichever modality has current credibility.
