## Ranked by threat

### 1. Master Tour (Eventric) — highest threat

- **Price:** $74.99/mo single-user.
- **Strengths:** Incumbent with TMs, 150K-contact network DB, offline mode (killer for touring), day sheets best-in-class.
- **Gaps:** No dispatch, no settlement, no AI, no transport, single-user, no deal pipeline, no email intelligence.
- **Threat signals to watch:**
  - Any mention of AI features in release notes.
  - Team/multi-user pricing introduction.
  - Acquisition by a larger ticketing/booking co.
- **Our response:** own the "AI for touring" narrative; publish demo content monthly.

### 2. FestivalPro

- **Price:** ~$45–499/mo + 2% transaction fee.
- **Strengths:** 600+ events, full lifecycle, ticketing, credentialing.
- **Gaps:** No transport, no AI, no free tier, no public API, festival-only.
- **Threat signals:** transport-vertical move, AI integration.
- **Our response:** festival dispatch + VRP is a moat here.

### 3. Lennd

- **Price:** Enterprise (est. $20K+/yr).
- **Strengths:** Credentialing, stakeholder portals, form collection.
- **Gaps:** No transport, no ROS anchors, no market intel, slow product cadence.
- **Threat signals:** lower-tier pricing, AI features.
- **Our response:** not overlapping yet; monitor.

### 4. Prism.fm

- **Price:** Enterprise.
- **Strengths:** Booking + deal memo pipeline, strong with agencies.
- **Gaps:** No DOS operations, no advance, no settlement for TMs.
- **Threat signals:** DOS/settlement vertical expansion.
- **Our response:** no overlap today.

### 5. Tourbase / Rehearsal

### 6. Artery (new entrant)

## Moats we're building

1. **Gmail + Claude intel layer.** 12–18 month window before copy.
2. **9-state advance + settlement stages.** Depth Master Tour won't match.
3. **Anchor-based ROS.** Technical moat that appears cosmetic.
4. **Cross-boundary: transport + advance + intel.** No competitor crosses all three.

## Gaps we must close

1. **Offline mode** (Master Tour parity).
2. **Contact DB** (network effect — takes years).
3. **Credentialing** (festival vertical table-stakes).
4. **Multi-tenant** (Platform work).
