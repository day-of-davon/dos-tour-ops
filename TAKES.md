# TAKES, calibration ledger

Append-only weekly predictions, scored the following week. The self-correcting
half of the consolidation loop: it turns the Weekly Operator Check into a
falsifiable record instead of a vibe.

Format: `- <date> | predict: <claim> | resolve-by: <date> | score(prev): <hit/miss/partial>`

- 2026-06-26 | predict: Olivia standing Sunday review booked | resolve-by: 2026-06-29 | score(prev): n/a (first entry)
